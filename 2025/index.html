MMVE 2025

March 31st- April 3rd — Stellenbosch, South Africa

Welcome to MMVE 2025

The 17th edition of the international workshop on IMmersive Mixed and Virtual Environment Systems (MMVE) will take place on March 31st-April 3rd, 2025 Stellenbosch, South Africa. It is co-located with MMSys 2025, a premier conference on multimedia systems.

About MMVE

Since 2018, MMVE has become a dynamic platform for experts from academia and industry to share insights on the rapidly evolving landscape of immersive media or extended reality (XR), including virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and mixed reality (MR), focusing on both the technological advancements and the societal implications of these immersive experiences.

In response to the increasing impact of immersive technologies, MMVE 2025 seeks to explore an essential and timely question: Who is being left out of these technologies? This theme invites researchers to reflect on accessibility, inclusivity, and the broader implications of immersive media. We encourage submissions that delve into these aspects, fostering dialogue on how to make these transformative technologies accessible to a wider audience.

Topics of Interest

We welcome contributions from diverse areas related to immersive systems, including but not limited to:

  • Immersive and Interactive Content;
  • Quality and Performance of Immersive Multimedia Systems;
  • Perception and Behaviour in Immersive Experience;
  • Support for Immersive Multimedia Systems;
  • Applied Contexts of Immersive Multimedia Systems;
  • Accessibility in XR.

Join us in Stellenbosch for an exciting exchange of ideas, collaboration, and the opportunity to shape the future of immersive technologies. Whether you are an experienced researcher, an industry professional, or new to the field, MMVE 2025 offers a platform to learn, network, and contribute to the growing body of knowledge in immersive systems.

We look forward to your participation!
